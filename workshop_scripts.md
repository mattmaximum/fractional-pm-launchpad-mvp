# High-Stakes Dad Energy Audit & Reset Workshop Scripts

## Day 1: The Audit

**Opening (0-5 min)**
- Welcome fellow engineers and TPMs. I see you - the guy who can optimize a distributed system but can't seem to optimize his own energy.
- Today we're doing a forensic energy audit. Think of it like profiling a slow API - we're going to find where your mental CPU is pegged at 100%.

**The Problem (5-15 min)**
- Context switching tax: Every time you jump from code to Slack to family, you pay a 23-minute focus recovery tax (cite UC Irvine study).
- By Thursday, your decision fatigue is so bad you're choosing the path of least resistance, not what's best for your energy.
- Physical stagnation: You're not lazy - your environment is optimized for sedentary work. Your body is literally seizing up.

**Energy Leak Detector Framework (15-45 min)**
Introduce the 3-category audit:
1. **Cognitive Leaks** (Where is your brain leaking focus?)
   - Context switching frequency
   - Decision fatigue points
   - Mental replay loops
   - Unclosed loops (that email you keep thinking about)

2. **Physical Leaks** (Where is your body bleeding energy?)
   - Sedentary hours vs movement
   - Sleep quality and consistency
   - Nutrition timing and quality
   - Recovery practices (or lack thereof)

3. **Emotional Leaks** (Where is your spirit getting drained?)
   - Guilt moments (work vs family)
   - Resentment buildup
   - Lack of boundaries
   - Energy vampires (people/tasks)

**Exercise: 90-Minute Audit (45-90 min)**
- Give them the worksheet with time-blocked audit
- Have them track: What were you doing? Energy level 1-10? What leaked?
- Look for patterns: Same time? Same activity? Same people?

**Closing (90-100 min)**
- Reveal: Most tech dads find 3 major leaks consuming 5-7 hours weekly.
- Preview Day 2: We're going to fix the biggest leak with a 15-minute movement protocol.
- Homework: Complete your audit and bring your top 3 leaks to tomorrow.

## Day 2: The Fix

**Opening (0-5 min)**
- Welcome back. By now you should have your top 3 energy leaks identified.
- Today we're attacking the physical leak first - because your body is the foundation. If your hardware is crashing, no amount of software optimization will work.

**Why Movement? (5-15 min)**
- Sedentary engineering work = systemic inflammation = brain fog.
- Your body wasn't designed for 8 hours of sitting and 4 hours of mental rumination.
- We're going to treat movement like a system optimization problem: minimum viable dose for maximum ROI.

**The 15-Minute Kettlebell/Club Reset (15-35 min)**
Introduce the protocol as a system:
- **Hardware Spec**: One kettlebell (24kg for men) or club (45cm, 2kg)
- **OS Requirements**: 15 minutes, 3x per week minimum
- **Core Movements** (the kernel level fixes):
  1. Kettlebell Swings (or Club Swings) - 5 min: Posterior chain activation, hip power, cardiovascular
  2. Turkish Get-Ups (or Club Mills) - 5 min: Full-body coordination, shoulder stability, mental focus
  3. Goblet Squats (or Club Squats) - 3 min: Leg drive, core tension, breathing control
  4. Farmer's Carry (or Club Carry) - 2 min: Grip strength, posture under load, walk tall

**Framing for Analytical Minds (35-50 min)**
- This isn't "working out" - it's preventive maintenance for your human hardware.
- Track it like a deployment: Reps, sets, weight, how you felt after.
- Neurological benefit: Complex movements require focus - it's a moving meditation that resets your prefrontal cortex.
- Hormonal benefit: Heavy compound movements spike testosterone and growth hormone - natural performance enhancers.

**Exercise: Movement Snack Programming (50-70 min)**
- Help them pick their 3x/week slots (suggest: Monday/Wednesday/Friday mornings)
- Have them choose their implement (kettlebell or club)
- Create their simple tracking spreadsheet: Date, Movements Completed, Energy Change (1-10)

**Closing (70-90 min)**
- Preview Day 3: We're going to build your Energy Fortress System - boundaries, rituals, and recovery protocols.
- Homework: Do your first 15-minute reset and note the immediate effect on your mental clarity.
